%A lay abstract of not more 150 words must be included explaining the key goals and contributions of the thesis in lay terms that is accessible to the general public. This page must be numbered ‘iii’.
The purpose of this paper is to define an MDE based approach to traceable variability between product families and requirements. This approach starts from the concept phase of a product idea and explores how to make traceable elements as a side effect of good engineering and model-based practices. It spans across goal diagrams, use case diagrams, requirement diagrams, and feature models.
